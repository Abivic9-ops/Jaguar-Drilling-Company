Our Services

Waziwa Drilling Company is a company that is dedicated to providing the outmost excellence in drilling machines and equipment to ensure you get your value for your money. We offer a variety of services.

Our Core Services

Borehole Drilling Services

We provide professional borehole drilling for residential homes, farms, institutions, commercial properties, and community water projects. Using modern drilling rigs and experienced technical teams, we ensure accurate depth targeting and stable borehole construction. Our process includes site preparation, drilling, casing installation, gravel packing, and proper sealing to protect groundwater from contamination. Every project is executed with safety, environmental care, and long-term performance in mind.

Hydrogeological Surveys & Site Assessments

Before drilling begins, we conduct thorough hydrogeological surveys to determine the most suitable drilling location. This includes geological mapping, water table analysis, and professional feasibility assessments. Our goal is to reduce risk, increase drilling success rates, and ensure sustainable water yield. Clients receive clear recommendations and transparent findings to support informed decision-making.

Pump Installation & Water System Setup

After successful drilling, we install reliable pumping systems tailored to your specific needs. This includes submersible pumps, solar-powered systems, electric pumps, storage tanks, and distribution connections. We assess water demand, energy availability, and long-term operational costs to design a system that is efficient, durable, and cost-effective. All installations are tested and calibrated before project handover.
